Our Story

About Noor

Mental Health Screening Platform for India

Noor (meaning "light" in Urdu/Hindi) was created with a simple vision: to make mental health screening accessible to every Indian, regardless of where they live or what they can afford.

Our Mission

We believe early screening is the first step toward healing. By providing free, clinically validated mental health tests in a stigma-free environment, we aim to bridge the gap between those who need help and those who can provide it.

Our Tests

All screening instruments used on Noor are based on internationally validated clinical tools — PHQ-9, GAD-7, PCL-5, ASRS, OCI-R, and more — adapted thoughtfully for the Indian context.

Privacy First

We do not store your personal health data on external servers. All records are stored locally on the device you use. You are always in control of your information.

Contact Us

For inquiries, partnerships, or feedback, write to us at hello@noor.health. We'd love to hear from you.

📚 Understanding Your Results

Screening scores are starting points, not endpoints. A high score means it's time to talk to someone — not that your life is defined by a number. Mental health exists on a spectrum and fluctuates over time.

🧘 Self-Help Strategies

  • Mindfulness & Breathing: Apps like Headspace and Calm offer guided sessions in Hindi and English.
  • Physical Activity: Even 30 minutes of walking daily can significantly reduce anxiety and depression symptoms.
  • Sleep Hygiene: Consistent sleep schedules are one of the most powerful mental health interventions.
  • Social Connection: Isolation worsens most mental health conditions. Reach out to at least one person today.

🏥 When to Seek Professional Help

If symptoms persist for more than 2 weeks, significantly impact your daily functioning, or involve thoughts of self-harm — please seek professional support immediately. You deserve care.
